Security Camera Guide

Turn any Android phone into a smart AI security camera.

What You Need

  • An Android phone (Android 8.0+) — even an old phone works great
  • A charger and power source (the phone needs to stay powered)
  • A way to prop or mount the phone
  • Wi-Fi connection (for Telegram alerts)

Phone Positioning

Best Placement

  • Corner of the room — widest field of view, covers entries
  • 0.5 to 1.5 meters high — captures faces, not just tops of heads
  • Facing the entrance — catch people as they enter
  • Landscape orientation — wider view (the app runs in landscape)
  • Use the ↩ 180° button if the phone needs to face the other direction

Avoid

  • Pointing at a TV or laptop screen — moving images trigger false detections
  • Direct sunlight on the lens — causes overexposure
  • Too high up (ceiling) — people look like blobs, AI can't identify humans
  • Behind glass — reflections cause issues, especially at night

Battery & Power

This is the #1 reason the app stops working. Samsung and other manufacturers kill background apps aggressively.

Required settings:

  • Settings → Apps → HumanDetector AI → Battery → Unrestricted
  • Keep the phone plugged in at all times
  • The app has power disconnect detection — if someone unplugs it, you get an alert
Use the Dim Screen button (or send /dim_on via Telegram) to save battery. The screen goes true black but the camera keeps running.

Startup Status Message

When the app starts and the detection engine is ready, it sends a status message to your Telegram:

HumanDetector AI — connection test OK S22 @ mobile (SM-S901E)

This shows the device name, location, and phone model — so you immediately know which phone just came online. When running multiple phones, each one announces itself with its own name and model info.

Detection Settings

Confidence: 50-55%

Lower = more sensitive (catches more, but more false positives). Higher = fewer false alarms.

Consecutive Frames: 3

Requires 3 frames of detection before alerting. Filters out single-frame glitches.

Face Verification: ON

Two-stage check — AI detects human shape, then verifies a face exists. Reduces false positives by 50-75%.

Motion Mode: Optional

Only runs AI when the scene changes. Saves battery and reduces heat on older phones.

Multi-Phone Setup

Use multiple phones to cover different areas. All phones use the same Telegram bot and chat ID.

  • Give each phone a unique Device Name (e.g., "kitchen", "frontdoor", "garage")
  • Send /help to see clickable commands for every connected phone
  • Send /status to see which phones are online and their battery levels
  • Use Heartbeat (e.g., every 15 minutes) to confirm phones are still running

Night Monitoring

The app works in low light but performance depends on your phone's camera quality. Tips:

  • Use a small night light or motion-activated light near the monitored area
  • The phone's flashlight can be toggled remotely: /torch_on_kitchen
  • Infrared doesn't work — phone cameras don't have IR LEDs

Use Cases

🏠 Home Security

Monitor entrances while away. Get photo alerts when someone enters.

🏢 Office After Hours

Leave a phone at the office. Motion mode saves battery during quiet hours.

🏖 Vacation Property

Leave a phone monitoring your AirBnB or holiday home. Check in remotely via /status and /photo.

📦 Package Watch

Point at your front door. Get alerted when the delivery person arrives.

🐕 Pet Monitor

Check in on pets via /photo command. Note: AI detects humans, not animals.

🏪 Small Business

Multiple phones covering store entrances and back rooms. All alerts in one Telegram chat.

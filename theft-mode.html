Theft Mode

Turn your phone into a self-defending security device that makes theft counterproductive.

What It Does

When someone picks up your phone, Theft Mode:

  • Detects the grab using the accelerometer
  • Gives a 15-second countdown (with warning beep) to enter the PIN
  • If PIN is not entered — activates full alarm: loud sound + flashlight strobe + rapid photos sent to your Telegram every 0.5 seconds
  • Photos alternate between front and back cameras to capture the thief's face AND surroundings
  • Auto-stops after your configured duration (1-5 minutes)

How It Works — Step by Step

📱

1. Grab Detected

Accelerometer detects sudden movement. A photo + alert is sent to Telegram immediately.

↓
⏱

2. Countdown (15 seconds)

Warning beep plays. Big red numbers on screen. PIN pad visible. Enter PIN to cancel.

↓ PIN not entered
🚨

3. Full Theft Mode

Maximum volume alarm + flashlight strobe + photos every 0.5s + camera alternates front/back every 5s. Every photo has a clickable /unlock command.

↓ PIN entered, /unlock tapped, or timeout
✅

4. Stopped

Alarm stops, strobe stops, photos stop. Phone returns to normal monitoring.

Power Disconnect Alert

Separate from grab detection — always active when Theft Mode is armed. If someone unplugs the charger:

  • 3-second grace period (avoids false alarm from accidental cable bumps)
  • If still disconnected after 3 seconds — sends a photo + "⚡ POWER DISCONNECTED" to Telegram
  • When power is restored — sends "🔌 Power restored"

The theft alarm tone is separate from the regular detection alarm tone. You can have a soft warning beep for regular detection and a loud fire alarm for theft.

How to Stop It

  • Enter PIN on screen — works during countdown or full theft mode
  • Tap /unlock in Telegram — every theft photo has a clickable /unlock command
  • Send /unlock_devicename via Telegram — e.g., /unlock_kitchen
  • Wait — auto-stops after configured duration (1-5 min)

Use Cases Beyond Security Cameras

Theft Mode works on any phone — it doesn't have to be a security camera. Here are ways to use it:

🏖 Phone on the Beach

Leave your phone on your towel while swimming. Arm theft mode. If someone grabs it — loud alarm + their face is sent to your friend's Telegram (set up a group chat).

☕ Phone at a Coffee Shop

Set your phone on the table, arm theft mode. Step away to get your coffee. If it moves — alarm + photos.

🏨 Hotel Room

Leave your phone facing the door with theft mode armed. If someone enters and moves the phone — instant alert with photos.

🚗 Phone in the Car

Leave an old phone on the dashboard. Arm theft mode. If the car is broken into and the phone grabbed — alarm + thief's photo sent to your Telegram.

Remote Commands

  • /theft_on_devicename — arm grab detection remotely
  • /theft_off_devicename — disarm remotely
  • /stolen_devicename — activate full theft mode immediately (skip countdown)
  • /unlock_devicename — stop theft mode and unlock phone
The /stolen command is for when you realize your phone was stolen after the fact. Send it from any Telegram device and the phone activates full alarm + starts sending photos immediately.

Tips

  • Always set a PIN before arming theft mode — otherwise there's no countdown, just immediate alarm
  • Place the phone on a stable surface — vibrating surfaces may cause false triggers
  • Keep Telegram configured — the photos are your evidence
  • The front camera captures the thief's face, the back camera captures the surroundings
  • Pin the /help message in Telegram for instant access to /unlock during an emergency
Theft Mode is a deterrent and evidence tool. It does not physically prevent theft. Always prioritize your personal safety over your phone.
